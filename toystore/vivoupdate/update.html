vivo Kicks off Android 14 Developer Preview Program for vivo X90 Pro and iQOO 11

Global technology company vivo today announced the release of the Android 14 Developer Preview Program for vivo X90 Pro and iQOO 11 in select markets. vivo, one of the first companies to make the Android 14 version available for eligible devices, invites developers to leverage this platform to test and fine-tune their applications for the latest Android experience.
